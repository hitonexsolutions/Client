Mainland Business Setup

Offshore Business Setup

When it comes to offshore companies, there is no place like the UAE. There are a number of nations that provide tax benefits and privacy safeguards for high-net-worth people’s assets and financial data. However, in recent years, Dubai offshore has become the safest and most advantageous tax haven for the establishment of new businesses. The Dubai offshore industry is unique among tax havens in that it has not signed any treaties obligating it to reveal its customers’ offshore financial dealings to their home nations. The Dubai offshore authority does not maintain a public registry of shareholders and directors, therefore you cannot be compelled to provide your business details to other nations or tax authorities.

Free Zone Business Setup

Are you hoping to do business in the Dubai Free Zone? The emirate is home to a number of free zones, including some of the most prominent in all of the United Arab Emirates. The business climate and availability of freehold land in several of these regions are exceptional. By locating their operations in the Dubai free zone, businesses may save money on customs duties and taxes.
